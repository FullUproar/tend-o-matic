## High-Level Product Thesis

Tend-O-Matic should treat compliance as a production-grade rules and evidence system, not as help text around checkout. The strongest implementation path is Michigan first, Illinois second, with a **Metrc-first integration model**, **versioned state-specific rule engine**, **operator-visible compliance explanations**, and **human-reviewed regulatory change pipeline**.

## Legal Caveat

This dossier is research and product strategy support, not legal advice. Before encoding values into production compliance controls or using customer-facing legal language, Tend-O-Matic should have cannabis-experienced counsel validate the state-specific requirements, contract clauses, banking posture, and entity structure.
